Our Story
More Than Just a Place to Eat

Girlfriend's Taphouse opened March 2024 at 225 Garrisonville Rd, bringing something Stafford was missing. General Manager Robert Shaw and partners Jen and Joe invested over $200,000 into a complete renovation of the space.

"An upscale casual restaurant. Not a dive bar, not corporate. Something Stafford doesn't have."

With over 20 staff members who treat every guest like family, we bring fresh flavors, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere for families, professionals, and everyone in between.
